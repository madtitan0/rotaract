Our Motives

Our Motives

Service and Community Impact

The primary motive of Rotaract clubs is to engage in meaningful service projects and make a positive impact in local and global communities. Rotaract members are motivated by a deep sense of service, working together to address societal needs, improve lives, and create lasting change.

Leadership Development and Personal Growth

Rotaract clubs provide a platform for young individuals to develop essential leadership skills and experience personal growth. Through hands-on project management, teamwork, and mentorship opportunities, Rotaractors enhance their leadership abilities, gain confidence, and expand their capabilities, setting a strong foundation for their future endeavors.

International Understanding and Cultural Exchange

Rotaract clubs foster international understanding and cultural exchange among members. Through collaboration with Rotaract clubs around the world, members have the opportunity to connect with diverse cultures, broaden their perspectives, and build lifelong friendships. This global network promotes peace, cooperation, and mutual understanding across borders.

Why Rotaract club?

Personal Growth

Joining a Rotaract club offers numerous opportunities for personal growth. You can develop leadership skills, enhance public speaking abilities, and improve your organizational and project management capabilities.

Professional Development

Rotaract clubs often provide platforms for networking with professionals from various fields. Engaging with like-minded individuals can help you build valuable connections, gain career insights, and even explore potential job opportunities.

Community Service

Rotaract clubs are committed to making a positive impact in their communities through various service projects. By joining, you have the chance to contribute to meaningful initiatives, address societal needs, and make a difference in the lives of others.

International Connections

Rotaract is a global organization, providing opportunities to connect with Rotaractors from different countries and cultures. You can participate in international conferences, exchanges, and collaborations, broadening your horizons and fostering cross-cultural understanding.

Friendship and Camaraderie

Rotaract clubs are known for their friendly and welcoming environments. By becoming a member, you can form lasting friendships with individuals who share similar values, interests, and a passion for service.

Fun and Social Activities

Rotaract is not only about service; it also offers a range of social activities and events. From social outings and team-building activities to workshops and conferences, Rotaract clubs provide a platform to have fun, relax, and create memorable experiences with fellow members.

FAQ

How long does it take to get started?

Our turnaround is lightning-fast. Just one week from signing off and we’re there with the kick-off meeting to get it rolling.

What is your typical working process?

It all starts with grabbing a cup of coffee, chilling with mates, and some quality time at the PlayStation (just kidding!). We always start a project with analysis, learning the requirements, and making estimates that will be shared in the price proposal. Then it’s off to signing off, kicking off, and getting your business to the next level with a pinch of our design magic.

Do you work on a fixed-price basis or time and material?

Flexibility is the key of success in this crazy world, so we try to suggest an appropriate solution for each specific case we deal with. The more clear and detailed the requirements are, the more accurate will our estimates be. This is the closest we get to the fixed-price model.

How do you usually start new collaborations?

Each new engagement starts with an introduction call and filling out the design/branding brief. If you have a cool project in mind, feel free to get in touch at muhammedriyaz.s2021@vitstudent.ac.in

Do you guys work with big companies only? We’re an early-stage startup.

We’re all for building a borderless world, so we are open to working with any project we come across. By the way, we do believe in startups, and one of those we helped develop from scratch has been sold to https://entercom.com/

How long it will take to get an estimate from you?

We hate to keep you waiting. Setting up a team, making preliminary research and analysis, and getting back to you with the contract will take up to 24 hours from the moment we learn what’s needed. Efficiency is one of key values.

Do you have a minimum engagement?

We set no entry thresholds for people who want to work with us. But we still believe that a product that needs to be designed can’t be made within a couple of days. After all, we need some time to research, analyze and come up with a strategy. So we’ve set a minimum project duration as 30 hours and we’re waiting for you!

What’s the average project duration?

Our team works full-time on every project, so the overall duration will highly depend on the initial estimate. On average, our projects last from 2 weeks to 5 months.

Can we start the design without wireframes?

Wireframes help to build UX without distractions. The wireframe is an essential part of the design process and we would not recommend eliminating it. Having everything polished at the wireframing stage, we can avoid many amendments and revisions at the design stage, which saves both time and money.

What templates does we use to create design?

We don’t use pre-built templates or kits because there’s just no soul in them. We’re committed to building fully customized and product-based solutions that will meet the expectations of the target users. Each pixel and line is designed with love and passion by our creative minds.

Where should I start with my business if I have an idea?

Hey, you’ve come to the right place! Please get in touch and we’ll help you with the ideation, design, and development. Full cycle on us, no hassle for you. Easy, right?
